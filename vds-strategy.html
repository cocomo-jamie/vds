Our Foundation and Expanded Horizons

  • Established August 2024: To build a local Data and AI community and provide a space for professional development.
  • Rapid Growth: Fueled by the evolution of AI, VDS has become a significant force in the local tech scene.
  • New Opportunities: Leveraging the national focus on AI to boost local economic growth and reduce dependence on the US.
  • Expanding Mandate: Broadening our focus to support local economic development for lasting societal benefit.

Our Vision and Mission

Vision: To establish Victoria as a premier data and AI innovation hub, recognized nationally and globally.

Mission: To align local data and AI talent with the needs of local organizations to drive innovation, build community, and grow the digital economy.

Our Strategic Objectives

  1. Build a Connected Community: Creating social and professional networks for collaboration and innovation.
  2. Support Professional Development: Offering opportunities for members to enhance their knowledge and advance their careers.
  3. Strengthen Partnerships: Collaborating with other non-profits for greater societal impact.
  4. Influence Local Economic Development: Strengthening the local economy and supporting business growth in the Data and AI space.

The NARA Practice: Connecting Needs and Resources

A platform cooperative to connect our ecosystem.

Three Key Pillars:

  • Resources: Our talented Data & AI professionals.
  • Needers: Organizations with data and AI challenges.
  • Orchestrators: Trusted partners for project delivery.

Value Proposition: We create a pipeline from need identification to solution delivery, fostering local economic development.

How to Engage: Membership and Partnership Models

For Individuals:

Tiered membership offering increasing levels of visibility and benefits, from a free listing to a full professional profile.

For Organizations:

  • Proposal-based engagement: Access our talent pool for specific projects.
  • Corporate Memberships (Silver, Gold, Platinum): Enhance brand awareness and connect with our community.

For Delivery Partners:

Flexible partnership models, including flat-fee and commission-based options.

Our Action Plan: From Short-Term Initiatives to a Long-Term Vision

Short-Term (0-1 Year):

  • VIATEC conference partnership in November 2025.

Medium-Term (1-3 Years):

  • Strengthened academic and non-profit partnerships.
  • Annual large-scale conferences.

Long-Term (3+ Years):

  • Establish Victoria as a leading Data and AI hub.
